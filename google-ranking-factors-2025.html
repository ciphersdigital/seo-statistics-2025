Google Ranking Factors in 2025

Google’s algorithm continues to evolve. Here are the most influential ranking factors in 2025, backed by industry data and expert consensus.

🚀 Top Factors

  • Page Experience: Mobile usability, HTTPS, page speed (Core Web Vitals)
  • Content Quality: Topical depth, freshness, and E-E-A-T signals
  • Backlinks: Still highly weighted, especially from authoritative sources
  • User Engagement: Bounce rate, CTR, time on page
  • Local Relevance: NAP citations, GMB, reviews
